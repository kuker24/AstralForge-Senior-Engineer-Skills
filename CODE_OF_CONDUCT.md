# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep collaboration respectful, evidence-based, and focused on improving the project.

Good participation includes:

- Being clear about what is verified and what is not.
- Respecting maintainers' time with small, reviewable changes.
- Giving constructive feedback with concrete examples.
- Avoiding personal attacks, harassment, discriminatory language, or intimidation.
- Avoiding pressure to expose secrets, private data, or credentials.

## Scope

This code applies to project spaces including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and maintainer communication related to this repository.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, close, or remove contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to blocked participation.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to a maintainer when possible. Include links, screenshots, or context when helpful, but do not include secrets or private credentials.
